Our College

Gafargaon Government College is a government college located in Gafargaon town of Mymensingh district. The college is located on the banks of the old Brahmaputra river in the city's college road. The college was established in 1950, converted into a degree college in 1965 and nationalized in 1985. This college has played an important role in the expansion of higher education in Gafargaon. This college was established 68 years ago to expand education in Gafargaon. The college was upgraded to a degree college in 1965. The college was officialized on 1st July 1985.
